## Positioning

ComplyScan wins on:
- AI-assisted remediation guidance (not just pass/fail checks)
- Fast free scans for acquisition
- Agency-friendly monthly plan for recurring usage
- Full report workflow in-browser (including Print / Save as PDF via browser)

## Competitor Gap

Most competitors offer one or more of:
- Rule-based checklist scans
- Cookie-only compliance checks
- CMP-focused tooling
- Informational SEO content

Most do not offer:
- Clear remediation instructions prioritized by risk
- Agency-specific unlimited scanning workflow
- Strong “show me how to fix this” messaging
