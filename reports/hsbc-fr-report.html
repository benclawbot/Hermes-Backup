[CRITICAL] Missing Privacy Policy Page

GDPR Articles 13 and 14 explicitly require controllers to provide comprehensive information to data subjects, including: identity and contact details of the controller, DPO contact, purposes and legal basis of processing, legitimate interests where applicable, recipients or categories of recipients, data retention periods, data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection), right to withdraw consent, right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, whether data is required for contractual purposes, and any automated decision-making/profiling. Without a published privacy policy, HSBC France cannot demonstrate compliance with these mandatory disclosure requirements.

Fix: Create a dedicated privacy policy page hosted at /privacy-policy or similar URL that comprehensively addresses all GDPR Article 13/14 requirements. The page must include: company identity (HSBC France), contact details, DPO contact information, detailed purposes of data processing, legal bases (consent, contract, legitimate interest), data recipient categories (including any third-country transfers and safeguards), retention periods for each data category, clear explanation of data subject rights, complaint rights, and whether automated decisions are made. Link this page prominently in the website footer on every page.

[WARNING] Cookie Banner Implementation Quality Uncertainty

While a cookie banner is present, the banner text appears to be a concatenated string without proper visual separation between sections ('Cookies présents sur ce siteNous utilisons des cookies pour garantir...'). This suggests the banner may not be displaying with proper formatting. Additionally, the banner mentions marketing cookies, which under GDPR require explicit opt-in consent (not opt-out). The current implementation claims to offer 'Accepter les cookies facultatifs' and 'Décliner les cookies facultatifs', but it is unclear whether non-essential cookies are truly blocked until explicit consent is obtained (prior consent requirement).

Fix: Verify that the cookie banner displays correctly with proper visual formatting and clear section separation. Ensure that prior to any user action, no non-essential cookies are set. Implement a true opt-in model where marketing and personalization cookies are only set after the user explicitly clicks 'Accepter les cookies facultatifs'. The 'Décliner les cookies facultatifs' option must result in those cookies not being set. Consider implementing a cookie preference center for granular consent control. Test that cookie settings persist correctly and can be modified later.
