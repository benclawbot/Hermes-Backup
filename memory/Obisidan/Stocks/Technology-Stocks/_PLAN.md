### Body sections (always include):
1. `# PAGE TITLE` (H1)
2. `> [!info] Summary` — one-sentence dense summary
3. `## Definition` — 2-4 paragraphs
4. `## Context and origin` — history, key companies, market evolution
5. `## Mechanisms / characteristics / details` — how it works, competitive dynamics, key metrics, players/shares
6. `## Nuances critiques limits` — risks, limitations, regulatory concerns, cyclicality
7. `## Links and implications` — how it connects to other pages
8. `## Sources` — [^1], [^2], [^3] citations

### Linking rules:
- `strong_links`: 3-5 most important dependencies (what this page needs to understand)
- `opposition_links`: alternative views or competing approaches
- In body text: `[[Page Title]]` wikilinks to related pages
- Links section: reference strong_links by name
- Every page must link to at least 3 other pages in the vault
